Create a Streamlit web app that converts electricity bills to Energybase's EXACT Excel format.

## CRITICAL UNDERSTANDING:

The company's Excel has:
- TWO consumers side by side (columns A-E for Consumer 1, columns G-J for Consumer 2)
- TWELVE months of historical data (Feb 2025 to Jan 2026)
- Formulas that calculate average, solar panels, system size
- Total solar capacity at bottom = sum of both consumers

Your app MUST generate Excel matching this EXACT structure.

## MULTIPLE BILL UPLOAD OPTION:

User can upload bills in TWO ways:

### Option 1: Upload ONE PDF
- MSEDCL bills often have 12 months on 1-2 pages
- AI will extract all 12 months from the PDF

### Option 2: Upload MULTIPLE images
- User can upload multiple files at once (Ctrl+Click or drag multiple)
- App processes all images sequentially
- Extracts data from each bill/photo
- Combines into ONE Excel with 12 months of data

Implementation: Use st.file_uploader with accept_multiple_files=True

## EXACT EXCEL FORMAT (MUST MATCH):

Columns A-E (Consumer 1):
- Row 2: "Consumer Name" | blank | [Name 1]
- Row 3: "Consumer No" | blank | [Number 1]
- Row 4: "Fixed Charges" | blank | 130
- Row 5: "Sanct. Load (kW)" | blank | [3.30KW]
- Row 6: "Connection Type" | blank | [90/LT I Res 1-Phase]
- Row 7: "Solar Pannel used" | 600 | blank
- Row 8: "Sr.No" | "Month" | "Units" | "Bill Amount" | "Unit Cost"
- Row 9-20: Monthly data (Feb 2025 to Jan 2026)
- Row 21: "Average" | blank | =AVERAGE(D9:D20) | =AVERAGE(E14:E20) | =AVERAGE(F14:F20)
- Row 22: "kW" | blank | =(D21*12*1.1)/1400 | blank | blank
- Row 23: "Solar Panels" | blank | =D22/$C$7*1000 | blank | blank
- Row 24: "Solar capacity" | blank | =ROUND(D23,0)*$C$7/1000 | blank | blank
- Row 25: "Number of Panels" | blank | =D24/$C$7*1000 | blank | blank

Columns G-J (Consumer 2):
- Row 2: blank | blank | blank | blank | blank | blank | [Name 2]
- Row 3: blank | blank | blank | blank | blank | blank | [Number 2]
- Row 4: blank | blank | blank | blank | blank | 130 | blank
- Row 5: blank | blank | blank | blank | blank | [1KW] | blank
- Row 6: blank | blank | blank | blank | blank | [Connection Type] | blank
- Row 8: blank | "Month" | "Units" | "Bill Amount" | "Unit Cost"
- Row 9-20: Monthly data for Consumer 2
- Row 21: "Average" | =AVERAGE(H9:H20) | =AVERAGE(I14:I20) | =AVERAGE(J14:J20)
- Row 22: "kW" | =(H21*12*1.1)/1400 | blank | blank
- Row 23: "Solar Panels" | =H22/$C$7*1000 | blank | blank
- Row 24: "Solar capacity" | =ROUND(H23,0)*$C$7/1000 | blank | blank
- Row 25: "Number of Panels" | =H24/$C$7*1000 | blank | blank

Bottom totals:
- Row 27: "Total solar capacity" | =sum(24:24) (across both consumers)
- Row 28: "Number of solar panels" | =sum(25:25) (across both consumers)

## WHAT AI MUST EXTRACT FROM BILLS:

For each bill/month, extract for both consumers:
- Month/Date (e.g., 2026-01-01)
- Units consumed (e.g., 25 for Consumer 1, 137 for Consumer 2)
- Bill amount (e.g., 320.45 for Consumer 1, 3335.34 for Consumer 2)
- Consumer names, numbers, sanctioned load, connection type (extract once, reuse)

If processing multiple images, COLLECT data across all images and combine into 12 months.
