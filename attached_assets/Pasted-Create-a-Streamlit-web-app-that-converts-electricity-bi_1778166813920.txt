Create a Streamlit web app that converts electricity bills to Excel in Energybase's specific format.

## WHAT THE APP DOES:
User uploads ONE bill (PDF or image) → AI extracts data → Downloads Excel file matching company's template exactly.

## THE EXCEL FORMAT (Must match exactly):

The Excel file must look like this:

Row 2: "Consumer Name" | blank | [Name 1] | blank | blank | blank | [Name 2]
Row 3: "Consumer No" | blank | [Number 1] | blank | blank | blank | [Number 2]  
Row 4: "Fixed Charges" | blank | 130 | blank | blank | blank | 130
Row 5: "Sanct. Load (kW)" | blank | [3.3KW] | blank | blank | blank | [1KW]
Row 6: "Connection Type" | blank | [text] | blank | blank | blank | [text]
Row 7: "Solar Pannel used" | 600 | blank | blank | blank | blank | blank
Row 8: "Sr.No" | "Month" | "Units" | "Bill Amount" | "Unit Cost" | "Month" | "Units" | "Bill Amount" | "Unit Cost"
Row 9-21: Monthly data (dates in column B and F, units in C and G)
Row 22: "Average" | =AVERAGE(D9:D21) | etc (LET EXCEL CALCULATE - DON'T FILL)
Row 23-29: Keep all existing formulas - DO NOT MODIFY

## WHAT AI MUST EXTRACT FROM THE BILL:

From one bill, extract for up to 2 consumers:

For Consumer 1:
- Name
- Consumer number  
- Sanctioned load (just the number, e.g., 3.3)
- Connection type
- Current month units (e.g., 25)
- Current month bill amount (e.g., 320.45)
- Current month date (e.g., 2026-01-01)

For Consumer 2 (if on same bill):
- Same fields as above
